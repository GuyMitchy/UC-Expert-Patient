Data Protection Information

1. Data Storage and Security

  • All health data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Data is stored in secure servers within the European Economic Area
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Multi-factor authentication for system access

2. Data Retention

  • Active account data: Retained while account is active
  • Inactive accounts: Data deleted after 12 months of inactivity
  • Chat logs: Retained for 6 months
  • Backup data: Encrypted and retained for 30 days

3. Data Processing Procedures

Your data is processed for:

  • Symptom tracking and analysis
  • Medication management
  • Food diary correlation
  • AI-powered chat support

4. GDPR Rights Implementation

You can exercise these rights at any time:

  • Right to access your data (Subject Access Request)
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restrict processing

5. Data Processors

We use these processors to deliver our service:

  • Heroku (hosting)
  • PostgreSQL (database)
  • OpenAI (chat functionality)

6. Data Breach Response

  • 24/7 monitoring for security incidents
  • 72-hour notification policy for affected users
  • Incident response team on standby
  • Regular breach simulation exercises

7. International Transfers

  • Data primarily stored in EU servers
  • Standard Contractual Clauses in place with processors
  • Regular compliance audits

8. Contact Information

For data protection inquiries:

  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@ucexpert.com
  • Response time: Within 72 hours
