User Guide

Getting Started

Quick Start Steps:

  1. Create your account
  2. Complete your profile
  3. Add current medications
  4. Log your first symptom

Tracking Symptoms

Record symptoms through the Symptoms tab:

  1. Click "Add Symptom"
  2. Select symptom type
  3. Rate severity (1-5)
  4. Add description (optional)
  5. Set date

Managing Medications

Keep track of your medications:

  • Add medications with dosage and frequency
  • Mark medications as active/inactive
  • Update medication details as needed
  • View medication history

Food Diary

Track your diet and identify triggers:

  • Log meals with date and time
  • Note portion sizes
  • Mark trigger foods
  • Add notes about reactions

Using the AI Chat

The AI Assistant can help with:

  • Understanding UC symptoms
  • Medication information
  • Diet suggestions
  • General UC management

Note: AI chat is not a substitute for medical advice.

Emergency Information

For severe symptoms:

  • Contact your healthcare provider immediately
  • Visit emergency services if needed
  • Do not rely on the app for emergency guidance

Tips for Best Use

Regular Tracking

Log symptoms and meals daily for better insights.

Detailed Notes

Add specific descriptions to track patterns.

Data Review

Review your logs before medical appointments.

Regular Updates

Keep medication information current.
